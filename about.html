Young Designer with Large Feet, 2020

I am a product designer

who loves making digital experiences that elicit audible holy molies.

I grew up in Dallas, studied psychology and architecture at Tulane University, backpacked across Asia and Europe, and found my way back to Dallas. Eager to continue my design education, I completed a product design apprenticeship with Bloc. This experience allowed me to leverage my foundations in psychology and architecture and grow as a user-centered designer operating in the digital realm. My design approach centers around merging data-validated user experiences with clean interfaces. I believe that thoughtful design holds tremendous power to explore, visualize and solve complex human issues.

In my free time, you can find me making art, living out my NBA dreams at the local rec center, honing my coding skills, and reassuring my dog that he’s a very good boy.

Please reach out! I’d love to connect.
